Translating Arabic Sign Language int Text

Sign language is the most important means of communication with deaf community and integrating them into the rest of society. Machine translation is one of the main research areas in natural language processing domain. Researchers have been working in this area for many decades. According to the World Health Organization in 2019, the number of people with hearing disability is around 466 million with 34 million of these are children. It is also estimated that this number is expected to double in the next 30 years. These numbers show the importance of sign language as a tool for communication between hearing impaired people and the rest of the world. Unfortunately, there is no universal sign language. as we focus on Arabic sign language, we found that there is no one standard Arabic sign language, but instead many nationals as many as the Arabic speaking countries.
